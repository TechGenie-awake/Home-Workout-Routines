Core Workout

Duration: 20-30 minutes

  • Plank: 30-60 seconds
  • Russian Twists: 15-20 reps per side
  • Leg Raises: 10-15 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 30 seconds
  • Side Plank: 20-30 seconds per side
  • Rest: 1 minute
  • Repeat for 2-3 rounds
