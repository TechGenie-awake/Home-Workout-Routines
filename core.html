Core

Workout

Strengthen and stabilize your core with targeted exercises designed to enhance your overall strength, balance, and posture.

Duration: 20–30 minutes | Rounds: 2–3 rounds | Rest: 1 minute between rounds

Warm-Up

(5 Minutes)

Get your core activated and ready with these dynamic warm-ups:

  • High Knees: 30 seconds
  • Torso Twists: 30 seconds
  • Cat-Cow Stretch: 5 cycles
  • Side Bends: 10 reps per side

Main Circuit

Perform each exercise in sequence, focusing on form and control. Complete 2–3 rounds.

01

Plank
(30–60 seconds)

  • Target Muscles: Core (abs, lower back), shoulders, and glutes.
  • Form:
    • Place forearms on the ground, elbows under shoulders.
    • Keep your body straight from head to heels.
    • Engage your core and avoid sagging hips.

02

Russian Twists
(15–20 reps per side)

  • Target Muscles: Obliques and abs.
  • Form:
    • Sit with knees bent and feet off the ground, holding a weight or ball.
    • Twist your torso side to side, touching the weight to the floor.
    • Keep your back straight and engage your core.

03

Leg Raises
(10–15 reps)

  • Target Muscles: Lower abs and hip flexors.
  • Form:
    • Lie on your back with legs extended.
    • Lift your legs until they are perpendicular to the ground, then slowly lower them.
    • Keep your lower back pressed into the floor.

04

Mountain Climbers
(30 seconds)

  • Target Muscles: Core, legs, and shoulders.
  • Form:
    • Start in a plank position.
    • Alternate driving your knees toward your chest quickly.
    • Maintain a flat back and engage your core.

05

Side Plank
(20–30 seconds per side)

  • Target Muscles: Obliques, shoulders, and core.
  • Form:
    • Lie on your side with your elbow directly under your shoulder.
    • Lift your hips to form a straight line from head to heels.
    • Hold the position and switch sides.

Cool Down

(5 Minutes)

Relax and stretch to aid recovery:

  • Cobra Stretch: 30 seconds
  • Child’s Pose: 1 minute
  • Seated Forward Bend: 30 seconds
