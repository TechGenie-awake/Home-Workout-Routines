Yoga

Flow

Reconnect your mind and body with this calming yet strengthening yoga flow. Ideal for enhancing flexibility, balance, and mindfulness.

Duration: 30–60 minutes | Level: Beginner to Intermediate

Warm-Up

(5 Minutes)

Start with gentle stretches to prepare your body for yoga:

  • Cat-Cow Pose: 1 minute
  • Child’s Pose: 30 seconds
  • Seated Forward Fold: 30 seconds
  • Neck Rolls: 30 seconds each side

Main Flow

Move through each pose mindfully, holding for 3–5 breaths unless otherwise stated.

01

Sun Salutation (Surya Namaskar)

  • Target: Full body stretch and warm-up.
  • Sequence:
    • Start in Mountain Pose (Tadasana).
    • Move through Forward Fold, Plank, Upward Dog, and Downward Dog.
    • Complete the cycle by returning to Mountain Pose.
  • Repeat: Perform 3–5 rounds.

02

Warrior II (Virabhadrasana II)

  • Target: Strengthens legs and opens hips.
  • Form:
    • Stand with feet wide apart and turn one foot outward.
    • Bend the front knee while keeping the back leg straight.
    • Stretch arms out to the sides and gaze over the front hand.
  • Hold: 5 breaths per side.

03

Tree Pose (Vrksasana)

  • Target: Improves balance and stability.
  • Form:
    • Stand on one leg, placing the other foot on your inner thigh or calf.
    • Bring palms together at the chest or raise arms overhead.
    • Keep your gaze steady to maintain balance.
  • Hold: 5 breaths per side.

04

Bridge Pose (Setu Bandhasana)

  • Target: Opens chest and strengthens glutes.
  • Form:
    • Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor.
    • Press into your feet to lift hips toward the ceiling.
    • Interlace fingers under your back if comfortable.
  • Hold: 5 breaths.

05

Corpse Pose (Savasana)

  • Target: Relaxation and mindfulness.
  • Form:
    • Lie on your back with arms relaxed by your sides.
    • Close your eyes and breathe deeply.
    • Let your body sink into the mat, releasing tension.
  • Duration: 5–10 minutes.

Cool Down

(5 Minutes)

Wrap up your session with gentle stretches:

  • Seated Spinal Twist: 30 seconds each side
  • Butterfly Pose: 1 minute
  • Happy Baby Pose: 1 minute
