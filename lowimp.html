Low-Impact

Cardio

Improve cardiovascular health, burn calories, and stay active without putting stress on your joints. This workout is ideal for all fitness levels, including beginners and those recovering from injuries.

Duration: 30–45 minutes | Rest: 15–30 seconds | Equipment: Optional

Warm-Up

(5–7 Minutes)

Start with gentle movements to prepare your body for exercise:

  • March in Place: 2 minutes
  • Shoulder Rolls: 1 minute
  • Side Steps: 1 minute
  • Arm Swings: 1 minute

Main Low-Impact Circuit

Perform each exercise at a steady pace. Take a 15–30 second rest between exercises and repeat the circuit 3–4 times.

01

Brisk Walking
(5 minutes)

  • Target: Cardio and endurance.
  • Tips:
    • Maintain a steady pace with upright posture.
    • Swing your arms naturally for momentum.

02

Step-Ups
(10–15 reps per leg)

  • Target: Glutes, quads, and calves.
  • Tips:
    • Use a sturdy step or platform.
    • Step up with one leg, then follow with the other.

03

Seated Marching
(1 minute)

  • Target: Lower body and core.
  • Tips:
    • Sit upright in a sturdy chair.
    • Lift your knees alternately, keeping your back straight.

04

Side Leg Raises
(10–15 reps per leg)

  • Target: Outer thighs and balance.

Cool Down

(5 Minutes)

Wrap up your session with gentle stretches:

  • Seated Hamstring Stretch: 30 seconds per leg
  • Neck Rolls: 30 seconds
  • Side Stretch: 30 seconds per side
  • Deep Breathing: 1 minute

Progression Over Time

Gradually increase the intensity and duration of your Low-impact workouts as you build strength and endurance. Here's how to progress over a 4-week period:

  • Week 1: Perform 3 rounds of the circuit with 30 seconds of rest between exercises.
  • Week 2: Increase to 4 rounds and reduce rest to 20 seconds.
  • Week 3: Perform 5 rounds with 15 seconds of rest between exercises.
  • Week 4: Add 5 seconds to each exercise and perform 5 rounds with 10 seconds of rest.
