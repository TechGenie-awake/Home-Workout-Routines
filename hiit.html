High-Intensity

Interval Training

Maximize results in minimum time with High-Intensity Interval Training. Short bursts of intense exercise followed by rest periods boost metabolism and burn calories effectively.

Duration: 20–30 minutes | Rounds: 4–5 rounds | Rest: 15–30 seconds

Warm-Up

(5 Minutes)

Start with a dynamic warm-up to prepare for intense movement:

  • High Knees: 1 minute
  • Arm Swings: 30 seconds
  • Side Lunges: 10 reps per side
  • Leg Swings: 10 reps per leg

Main HIIT Circuit

Perform each exercise with maximum intensity. Rest for 15–30 seconds between exercises. Repeat the circuit 4–5 times.
