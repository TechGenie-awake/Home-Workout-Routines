Full-Body Circuit

Duration: 30-45 minutes

  • Push-ups: 10-15 reps
  • Squats: 15-20 reps
  • Lunges: 10-15 reps per leg
  • Plank: 30-60 seconds
  • Burpees: 10 reps
  • Rest: 1-2 minutes
  • Repeat for 3-4 rounds
